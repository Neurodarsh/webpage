About Dr. Darshan

Dr. Darshan Belur Yatheesh is a distinguished neurosurgeon with an M.Ch in Neurosurgery. He is committed to providing excellent and compassionate care, leveraging advanced surgical techniques and a patient-first approach to address a wide range of neurological conditions.

With extensive experience in both complex brain and spine surgeries, Dr. Yatheesh is dedicated to staying at the forefront of his field through continuous education and adoption of the latest medical technologies.

He believes in a collaborative model of care, ensuring each patient and their family are fully informed and active participants in their treatment journey, from diagnosis through recovery.

Areas of Expertise

Brain Tumor Surgery

Specializing in the resection of both benign and malignant brain tumors, including gliomas, meningiomas, and pituitary adenomas.

Complex Spinal Surgery

Expertise in treating a wide range of spinal conditions, from degenerative disc disease to spinal deformities and tumors.

Vascular Neurosurgery

Proficient in surgical and endovascular treatments for cerebral aneurysms, arteriovenous malformations (AVMs), and stroke.
